About Me

I am a Mathematical Finance student at Hofstra University and a member of the Rabinowitz Honors College, pursuing minors in Information Systems & Business Analytics, Accounting, and Economics. My academic work focuses on data analytics, statistical reasoning, and machine learning through coursework and independent study. I have built strong technical skills in Python, data visualization, and financial analysis, which help me interpret complex data and communicate clear insights. Outside the classroom, I founded PRIME, Hofstra’s Artificial Intelligence Association, to create hands-on learning opportunities around AI and analytics. I am interested in combining finance, data science, and cloud technologies to solve real-world business problems in structured analytical settings.
